CHRIST VIDYANIKETHAN SCHOOL (ICSE)



Christ Vidyanikethan, Irinjalakuda is run by theChrist Educational and Charitable Trust (Reg. No. 449/SRO/E.K.M/2002), under the aegis of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI). It strives to realise the vision of Blessed Father Kuriakose Elias Chavara, prominent educationalist and social reformer of the 19th century and founder of the CMI congregation.

                                                            


PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE



Christ beckons you! Christ Vidyanikethan, is the most sought after school, for it enfolds the curriculum to encompass the talents of all the students to succeed and reach their true potential both in scholastic and co-scholastic realms. It is inspiring to note that the wellbeing of students is our top priority and we strive to personalize educational process to bring out the best in each learner to be excellent performing citizen. Fr. Joy Alappat CMI Principal
